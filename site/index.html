What is Challanghyah?

Challanghyah is a website to challenge you daily, weekly or even yearly to do things you usually wouldn't do. Challanghyah can be used from using it for your workout, not having sugar for a week, to stopping smoking. The possibilities are endless! But it is 100% FREE *
